Flex Cable Locations For iPads

It is important to know where the flex cable is located before you try to perform any repair to your device, depending on the model, you need to open the iPad from a certain direction and to a certain degree to avoid damaging the flex cable. Here is a quick reference to where the the flex cable is for some popular iPad models:

Model NameModel NumberFlex Cable PositionHow to Open
Air 2A1566, A1567Bottom RightLift from the top side, up to 60 degrees
Air 3A2123, A2152, A2153Lower CenterLift from the top side, up to 45 degrees
Air 4A2072, A2316, A2324, A2325Lower RightLift from the left side
Mini 4A1538, A1550Bottom RightLift from the top side, up to 60 degrees
Mini 5A2124, A2126, A2133Bottom RightLift from the top side, up to 60 degrees
Pro 9.7“A1673, A1674, A1675Lower RightLift from the left side
Pro 10.5“A1701, A1709Lower CenterLift from the top side, up to 45 degrees
Pro 11.0“ 1st GenA1934, A1980, A2013Top Center(Face ID); Left (LCD)Slide LCD down slightly to disconnect face ID flex at top. Open from the Right side
Pro 11.0“ 2nd GenA2068, A2228, A2230Top Center(Face ID); Left (LCD)Slide LCD down slightly to disconnect face ID flex at top. Open from the Right side
Pro 11.0“ 3rd GenA2377, A2459, A2301, A2460Top Center(Face ID); Left (LCD)Slide LCD down slightly to disconnect face ID flex at top. Open from the Right side
Pro 12.9“ 1st GenA1584, A1652Lower CenterLift from the top side, up to 45 degrees
Pro 12.9“ 2nd GenA1670, A1671Lower CenterLift from the top side, up to 45 degrees
